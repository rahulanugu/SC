Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scriptchain?

ScriptChain is an AI based platform that helps physicians with identifying high risk patients of readmission and cardiovascular disease with actionable insights. We are a plugin to the major EMR systems that helps physicians save time and generate more revenue for their facility.

Who are the users?

Our main users are primary care physicians and cardiologists.

Is this service free?

We have a software as a service model so we charge customers based on their usage bracket but are looking for beta volunteers to try out the platform for free for the first 50 patients!

When are we going to launch?

We are looking to launch officially nationwide early 2022.

Why is our domain tailored towards cardiovascular diseases?

Cardiovascular disease is the #1 cause of death not just in the United States but in the entire world accumulating to around 18 million deaths in 2017 alone and the figures are growing annually. Since we are in preventive medicine we believe we can make a significant difference in people's lives by intervening in the disease before patients are diagnosed and looking to lower the readmission rate in the nation that costs the healthcare system around $500M a week.
